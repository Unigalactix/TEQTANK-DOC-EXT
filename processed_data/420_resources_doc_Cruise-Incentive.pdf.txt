PROGRAM DETAILS AND RULES
frequense. Cruise
You must earn a minimum of 10,000 points to participate in the program and a maximum of 43,000 points. All points over 42,000 will be forfeited. A maximum of one cabin may be earned.
Points are non-transferable, have no cash value and may not be carried over to the next program.
VIP Club Details - Affiliates may purchase one VIP Membership at a cost of $100 prior to April 1, 2025. Affiliates will receive 1,000 points. VIP Members will have the opportunity to make their cabin requests 7 days prior to all qualified Affiliated, One Exclusive Point Promo offered only to VIPs and Exclusive VIP shirt for those who attend. This purchase is non-refundable and non-transferable. Other perks may apply.
Affiliates must use points towards the purchase of their Cabin prior to purchasing any upgrade or additional options.
Affiliates who qualify to attend may purchase additional passages for family members within the same cabin. Cabins that hold 3 or 4 passengers are limited to Inside and Balcony and are very limited. Requesting these special cabins is based on availability.
The cruise line does not allow infants under the age of 6 months or women who are on or past their 24th week of pregnancy.
Affiliates purchasing a single passage and traveling without a guest, will be roomed with another Affiliate. Single guests will only be allowed to upgrade if there is another single guest requesting an upgrade. You will have the option of requesting a roommate.
This package includes an inside cabin category 3V, port charges and taxes, Frequense special meetings/events and Frequense Gifts.
Qualified Affiliates will be notified within 7 days after the promotion ends. VIP members have until November 15 to make their priority requests. All other qualified Affiliated have until November 30th to submit their participation requests. A non-refundable deposit of $100 per person will be required regardless of if the entire trip has been earned. This deposit will be applied to balance due, shipboard account or refunded on the trip.
All balances must be paid in full no later than December 31, 2025.
Point Category Descriptions
Activity Points - These points are based on retail amount of commisionsionable items or- dered during the month unless otherwise noted. Non-commissionable items are excluded.
Personal Orders - You'll receive 1 pt. for every dollar spent on orders placed from your personal account.
Personal New Customer/Affiliate Volume - You'll receive 1 pt. for every dollar spent on all orders placed from your new Customers and Affiliates during their signup month.
Personal Paid Rank (25 pts. - 800 pts.)
These points are issued monthly based on your paid rank for the month. See chart for monthly points per rank.
BUSINESS BUILDING POINTS Promote a Personal New Gold Affiliate (250 pts.) - These points are issued when you help one of your personally enrolled advance in rank to Gold Affiliate.
VIP Membership Points - You will receive 1,000 points for joining the VIP Incentive Club. See membership details below.
Special Activity Points - These points will be given for special promotions that will be offered throughout the incentive.
