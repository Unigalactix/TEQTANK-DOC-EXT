PROGRAM DETAILS & RULES
You must earn a minimum of 10,000 points to participate in the program. A maximum of one cabin may be earned.
Points are non-transferable, have no cash value and may not be carried over to the next program.
VIP Club Details - Affiliates may purchase one VIP Membership at a cost of $100 prior to April 1, 2025. VIP membership includes 1000 points, 7 day advance cabin selection, one exclusive point promotion, and VIP Shirt for those who attend the cruise. This purchase is non-refundable and non-transferable. Other perks may apply.
Affiliates must use points towards the purchase of their Cabin prior to purchasing any upgrade or additional options.
Affiliates who qualify to attend may purchase additional passages for family members within the same cabin. Cabins that hold 3 or 4 passengers are limited to Inside and Balcony and are very limited. Requesting these special cabins is based on availability.
The cruise line does not allow infants under the age of 6 months or women who are on or past their 24th week of pregnancy.
Affiliates purchasing a single passage and traveling without a guest, will be roomed with another Affiliate. Single guests will only be allowed to upgrade if there is another single guest requesting an upgrade. You will have the option of requesting a roommate.
This package includes an inside cabin category 3V, port charges and taxes, Frequense special meetings/events and Frequense Gifts.
Qualified Affiliates will be notified within 7 days after the promotion ends. VIPmembers will be able to start making their requests on August 20. All other qualified Affiliated can start their requests on October 15. Reservations will close on December 1. A non-refundable deposit of $200 per person will be required to select your cabin. This deposit will be applied to any balance due. If there is no balance due, this deposit will be refunded on the trip. The second deposit of 75% of the total remaining balance is due by November 19. Final balances must be paid in full by January 5, 2026.
Travel and Onboard credits - Travel and Onboard funds can be requested after the purchase of a cabin and any upgrades. There is a maximum of 5,000 points for one person or 10,000 points for two people or no more than the actual cost of travel for Travel Credit. There is a maximum of 2,000 points for one person or 4,000 points for two people for Onboard Credit.
Cancellation Policy -The $200 per person deposit is non-refundable. Cancellation request made 12/7/25 will receive their deposits minus the $200 pp non-refundable amount. Cancellations requests between 12/8/25 - 1/5/26 will receive 25% of the refundable deposit back. There are no refunds issued after 1/5/26.
